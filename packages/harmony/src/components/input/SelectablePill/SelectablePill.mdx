<Heading>
## Overview

The Selectable Pill component is used for binary selections. They
do not trigger a popover menus and therefore have no active state, but are
used to signal a selection. They’re seen during sign-up and on the Audius
Feed, Trending, and the top of the Library.

</Heading>
